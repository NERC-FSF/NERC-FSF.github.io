    NEODAAS-FSF Remote Sensing Workshop 2024

    NERC FSF, working with the National Earth Observdation Data Acquisition and Analysis Service, will once again be hosting our highly popular Remote Sensing Workshop in 2024.

    This event will include hands on use of a field spectroradiometer, analysing field spectrometer data, processing the acquired spectra in Python, and using the spectral products gained to conduct supervisied classification of a UAV imagery.

    The course will be head at the National Space Park in Leicester from the 14th to the 17th May.

    If interested, contact the facility at fsf@ed.ac.uk for more details.
