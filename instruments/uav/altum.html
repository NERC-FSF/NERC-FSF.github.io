    Overview

    The MicaSense Altum is a lightweight, multispectral camera allowing for the synchronized capture of highresolution multispectral, panchromatic, and thermal imagery. Thermal and multispectral outputs are calibrated by the use of a reflectance panel and downwelling light sensor (DLS 2), which includes an integrated GPS unit.

    Technical Specification

    Value
    Spectral Bands (with centre wavelength) Blue (475 nm), Green (560 nm), Red (668 nm), Red edge (717 nm), Near infrared (840 nm), and Thermal (11 µm)
    Sensor Resolution 2064 x 1544 (3.2 MP per EO Band) at 120 m; 81 cm per Pixel at 120 m (Thermal)
    Ground Sampling Distance 5.28 cm per Pixel (per EO Band) at 120 m (AGL); 81 cm per Pixel at 120 m (Thermal)
    Field of View Multispectral: 50.2° x 38.4°; Thermal: 57° x 44°
    Focal Length Multispectral: 0.8 cm; Thermal: 0.2 cm
    Dimensions 8.2 x 6.7 x 6.75 cm
    Weight 0.41 kg
    External Power 4.9 to 25.2 V
    Power Input Standby: 5.5 W; Average: 7.0 W; Peak: 10 W
