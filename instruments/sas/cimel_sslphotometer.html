    Overview

    The Cimel Cimel Electronique CE318-TS9 Sun-Sky-Lunar Photometer is a portable automatic tracking photometer measuring sun, sky and lunar luminances in 8 filters over visible to near infrared wavelengths.

    The CE CE318-TS9 is fitted with eight filters for measuring atmospheric aerosol optical thickness, and an additional filter at 939 nm for measuring atmospheric water vapour.

    The instrument automatically computes the position of the sun and tracks its movement. It can be programmed to collect data in manual or automated sequences of measurements, which include almucantar and principal plane scenarios.
