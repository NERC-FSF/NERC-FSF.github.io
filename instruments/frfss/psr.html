    Overview

    The Spectral Evolution PSR+ 3500 is a full range single-beam field spectroradiometer measuring over the visible to short-wave infrared wavelength range (350-2500nm). The subminiature assembly (SMA) mating on the fibre optic accessory for the PSR+ 3500, combined with an extensive array of accessories, makes it ideal for customizable setups for field spectroscopy, including interfacing with the FSF Absorption Spectroscopy Kit.

    The PSR+ 3500 can be operated in stand alone mode using the in built graphical user display at the rear of the unit, or via wired or wirless connection to a Windows OS computer with Spectral Evolution’s DARWin SP data acquisition software installed. DARWin SP also comes pre-installed with a spectral library of mineral reflectances, as well as the ability to conduct calculation of basic spectral indices, such as NDVI, on acquired spectra.

    The instrument can be operated in nadir mode using one of the two (4° and 8°) foreoptic lenses, or – when using the SMA connector fibre optic extension – attached to a bundled contact probe, as well as to any device or accessory which accepts SMA connectors1

    The PSR+ 3500 also comes with an external light source and fibre contact probe, allowing for mm spot size contact measurements.

    Technical Specification

    Value
    Spectral range 350 nm – 2500 nm
    Sampling Interval 1.5 nm between 350 nm – 1000 nm; 3.8 nm between 1000 nm – 1890 nm; 2.5 nm between 1890 nm – 2500 nm
    Spectral Resolution 3.0 nm at 700 nm; 10.0 nm at 1500 nm; 8.0 nm at 2100 nm
    Scanning Time Automatic with programmable options or manual control. Typical multi-scan acquisition time <10 seconds
    Fore Optic Lenses - Field of View 4°, 8°
    Fore Optic Accessories High intensity contact probe for geological samples; Reflectance probe for mm spot size contact measurements; leaf clip; SMA fibre optic extension
    Power source Interchangeable 7.4V lithium ion rechargeable battery packs; mains power adaptor
    Weight 3.5 kg
    Size 21.6cm x 29.2 cm x 8.3 cm
